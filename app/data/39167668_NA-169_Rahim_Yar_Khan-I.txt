NA-169 Rahim Yar Khan-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Makhdoom Syed Ahmad Alam Anwar an Independent candidate won by 74,933 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Syed Hamid Saeed Kazmi of PPP won by 65,395 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Khwaja Ghulam Rasool Koreja of PPP won by 80,499 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == ==See also== *NA-168 Bahawalpur-V *NA-170 Rahim Yar Khan-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-192
