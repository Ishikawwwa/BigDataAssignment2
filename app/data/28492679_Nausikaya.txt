Nausikaya (Nausikaja) is a 1995 Croatian film directed, written and produced by Vicko Ruić. It is based on "The Spider", a horror short story by Hanns Heinz Ewers. The film was selected as the Croatian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 69th Academy Awards, but was not accepted as a nominee.Margaret Herrick Library, Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences ==Cast== * Nada Gačešić as Marija Slajner (as Nada Gacesic-Livakovic) * Igor Serdar as Matija Remetin * Maja Nekić as Nausikaja * Mustafa Nadarević as Inspector Stevovic ==See also== * List of submissions to the 69th Academy Awards for Best Foreign Language Film * List of Croatian submissions for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film ==References== ==External links== * Category:1995 films Category:Serbo-Croatian-language films Category:Films based on short fiction Category:Croatian drama films Category:Films set in 1939 Category:Films set in Zagreb Category:1995 directorial debut films Category:1995 drama films
