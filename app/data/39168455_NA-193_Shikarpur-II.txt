NA-193 Shikarpur-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-199 Shikarpur-II=== Election Member Party 2018 Ghos Bakhsh Khan Mahar GDA == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Ghous Bux Khan Mahar of PML-Q won by 61,432 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Ghous Bux Khan Mahar of PML-Q won by 89,921 votes defeating Sardar Wahid Bakhsh Bhayo of PPP who received 39123 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013.
