Nitilsukh (; ) is a rural locality (a selo) in Saniortinsky Selsoviet, Tlyaratinsky District, Republic of Dagestan, Russia. Population: == Geography == Nitilsukh is located 16 km southeast of Tlyarata (the district's administrative centre) by road.
