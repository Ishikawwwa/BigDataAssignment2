Naranjales (Naranjales barrio) is a barrio in the municipality of Mayagüez, Puerto Rico. Its population in 2010 was 1109. ==History== Puerto Rico was ceded by Spain in the aftermath of the Spanish–American War under the terms of the Treaty of Paris of 1898 and became an unincorporated territory of the United States. In 1899, the United States Department of War conducted a census of Puerto Rico finding that the combined population of Naranjales barrio and Río Cañas Arriba barrio was 1,048. ==See also== * List of communities in Puerto Rico ==References== Category:Barrios of Mayagüez, Puerto Rico
