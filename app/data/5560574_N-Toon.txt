N-Toon was an R&B; group from Atlanta created by former Klymaxx frontwoman Joyce Irby in 1996. The group consisted of Lloyd Polite, Justin Clark, Everett Hall and Chuckie D. Reynolds. The group disbanded in 2001. Toon Time was released on DreamWorks in March 2000. Later that year, MCA Records crossed over into its parent company, Geffen Records.
