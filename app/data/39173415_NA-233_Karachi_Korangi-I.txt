NA-233 Karachi Korangi-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan that covers Shah Faisal Colony, and Model Colony. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-239 Karachi Korangi-I=== Election Member Party 2018 Muhammad Akram PTI 2022 Imran Khan PTI == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Iqbal Muhammad Ali Khan of Muttahida Qaumi Movement won by 39,196 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Iqbal Muhammad Ali Khan of Muttahida Qaumi Movement won by 123,491 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Iqbal Muhammad Ali Khan of Muttahida Qaumi Movement won by 151,788 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections were held on 25 July 2018. == By-election 2022 == A by-election was held on 16 October 2022 due to the resignation of Muhammad Akram, the previous MNA from this seat. Imran Khan won by a margin of 24,625 votes. == By-election 2023 == A by-election will be held on 28 May 2023 due to the vacation of this seat by Imran Khan, who won it in the 2022 by-election. ==See also== *NA-232 Karachi Malir-III *NA-234 Karachi Korangi-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-256 Karachi
