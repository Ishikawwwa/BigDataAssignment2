N'Kosie Barnes (born 17 December 1974) is a former sprinter from Antigua and Barbuda who competed in the 1996 Summer Olympics and the 2000 Summer Olympics. In 1996, he was a part of the Antiguan men's 4 × 400 m relay that did not advance from their heat.
