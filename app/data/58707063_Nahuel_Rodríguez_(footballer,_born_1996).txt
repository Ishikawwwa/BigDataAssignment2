Nahuel Jonathan Rodríguez González (born 18 March 1996) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder. ==Career== Rodríguez's first senior career club was Argentinos Juniors, who he started appearing for during the 2015 Argentine Primera División season. Manager Néstor Gorosito picked Rodríguez three times that campaign, which included his professional bow during a 1–0 defeat to Racing Club on 26 September. In May 2017, Estudiantes of Primera B Metropolitana signed Rodríguez on loan. He remained until the end of 2017, featuring in both 2016–17 and 2017–18. Soon after returning to Argentinos Juniors, Rodríguez was loaned out again as he joined Paraguayan Primera División side Deportivo Capiatá. ==Career statistics== .
