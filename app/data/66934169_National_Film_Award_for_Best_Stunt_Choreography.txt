The National Film Award for Best Stunt Choreography is one of the National Film Awards presented annually by the Directorate of Film Festivals, the organisation set up by Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, India. It is one of several awards presented for feature films and awarded with Rajat Kamal (Silver Lotus). The award was instituted in 2016, at 64th National Film Awards and awarded annually for films produced in the year across the country, in all Indian languages. == Winners == Award includes 'Rajat Kamal' (Silver Lotus) and cash prize.
