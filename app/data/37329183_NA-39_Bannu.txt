NA-39 Bannu () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It covers the whole of district Bannu. The constituency was formerly known as NA-26 Bannu from 1977 to 2018. The name changed to NA-35 Bannu after the delimitation in 2018. ==Members of Parliament== ===1977–2002: NA-26 Bannu=== Election Member Party 1977 Sahibzada Saifullah PNA 1985 Sahibzada Fateh-ullah Independent 1988 M.
