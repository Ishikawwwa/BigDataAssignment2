NA-189 Rajanpur () is a newly-created constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It mainly comprises the Rojhan Tehsil and the town of Kot Mithan. It was created in the 2018 delimitation from the bifurcation of the old NA-175. == Election 2018 == Generel Elections were held on 25 October 2018.
