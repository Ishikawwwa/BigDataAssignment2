N'abania is the debut studio album by Nigerian singer Flavour N'abania, released in 2005. The album comprises 12 tracks and features guest appearances from Mr Raw, Skit, Prophecy and D Mustard. ==Singles== "N'abania" was released as one of the album's singles, and features guest vocals by Mr Raw. The song's lyrics addresses the expectations of nightlife events. Nigerian musician J. Martins made a cameo appearance in the music video. "Nwa Baby" was also released as one of the album's singles.
