Nuestra Belleza Michoacán 2011, was held in the Plaza Morelos of Uruapan, Michoacán on June 30, 2011. At the conclusion of the final night of competition, Edna Álvarez of Nueva Italia was crowned the winner. Álvarez was crowned by outgoing Nuestra Belleza Michoacán titleholder, Karla Paulina Gutiérrez.
