N-succinylornithine carbamoyltransferase (, succinylornithine transcarbamylase, N-succinyl-L-ornithine transcarbamylase, SOTCase) is an enzyme with systematic name carbamoyl phosphate:N2-succinyl-L-ornithine carbamoyltransferase. This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction : carbamoyl phosphate + N2-succinyl-L-ornithine \rightleftharpoons phosphate + N-succinyl-L-citrulline This enzyme is specific for N-succinyl-L-ornithine. == References == == External links == * Category:EC 2.1.3
