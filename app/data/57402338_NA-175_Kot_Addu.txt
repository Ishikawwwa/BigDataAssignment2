NA-175 Kot Addu () is newly created a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It mainly consists of the city of Kot Addu and the towns of Dera Din Panah and Chowk Sarwar Shaheed of Kot Addu District, areas which were previously in the old NA-176 before the 2018 delimitations. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018.
