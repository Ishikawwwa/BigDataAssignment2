NA-70 Sialkot-V () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It mainly comprises the Sambrial Tehsil. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-76 Sialkot-V=== Election Member Party 2018 Shamim Ahmed PML (N) == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Umar Ahmad of PML-Q won by 68,468 votes and Salman Saif Cheema of PPP got 31,602 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Rana Abdul Sattar son of Rana Shamim Ahmed Khan of PML-N won by 92,182 votes against the president of PMLQ Ch Shujaat Hussain. == Election 2013 == General election 2013 were held on May 11, 2013. Rana Shamim Ahmed Khan won by 129,571 votes and became the member of National Assembly.
