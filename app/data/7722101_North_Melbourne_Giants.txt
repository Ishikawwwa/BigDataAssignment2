The North Melbourne Giants, previously known as the Coburg Giants, were an Australian professional basketball team based in Melbourne. The Giants competed in the National Basketball League (NBL) between 1980 and 1998, and played their home games at The Glass House. The Coburg Giants entered the league in its second season (1980) and would change their name to the North Melbourne Giants in 1987.
