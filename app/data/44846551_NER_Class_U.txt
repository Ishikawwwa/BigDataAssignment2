The NER Class U (LNER Class N10) was a class of 0-6-2 tank locomotives of the North Eastern Railway. It was designed by Wilson Worsdell and introduced in 1902. ==Use== The locomotives were used for shunting and on goods trains. All were fitted with Westinghouse brakes. ==Numbering== The whole class was transferred from the NER to the London and North Eastern Railway (LNER) in 1923 and to British Railways (BR) in 1948.
