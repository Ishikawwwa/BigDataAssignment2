The street runs about 400 meters from north to south and connected to Hondori at the north end and Peace Boulevard at the south end. ==History== * The street used to be a part of a canal for the castle town, and the canal was called "Hirataya-gawa (Hirataya River)". * In 1915 and 1955, the river (canal) was reclaimed.
