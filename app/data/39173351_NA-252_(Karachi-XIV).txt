NA-252 (Karachi-XIV) () was a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. After the 2018 delimitations, its area has been divided between NA-243 (Karachi East-II) and NA-244 (Karachi East-III), while NA-245 (Karachi East-IV) consists entirely of the western part of NA-252. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Abdul Rasheed Godil of Muttahida Qaumi Movement won by 91,339 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Abdul Rasheed Godil of Muttahida Qaumi Movement won by 87,280 votes. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Muhammad Hussain Mehanti of Muttahida Majlis-e-Amal won by 33,089 votes. == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-252 Category:Abolished National Assembly Constituencies of Pakistan
