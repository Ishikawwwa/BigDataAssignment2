Novokizganovo (; , Yañı Kiźgän) is a rural locality (a village) in Tazlarovsky Selsoviet, Burayevsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 491 as of 2010. There are 12 streets. == Geography == Novokizganovo is located 23 km northeast of Burayevo (the district's administrative centre) by road.
