NA-154 Lodhran-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Muhammad Akhtar Khan Kanju of PML-Q won by 103,209 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Hayatullah Khan Tareen of PPP won by 50,490 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Abdul Rehman Khan Kanju of PML-N won by 85,452 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == ==See also== *NA-153 Multan-VI *NA-155 Lodhran-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-155
