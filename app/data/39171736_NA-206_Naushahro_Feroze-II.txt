NA-206 Naushahro Feroze-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Abdul Ghaffar Khan Jatoi of National Alliance won by 83,043 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Ghulam Murtaza Khan Jatoi of National Peoples Party won by 109,319 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Ghulam Murtaza Khan Jatoi of National Peoples Party won by 83,960 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-205 Naushahro Feroze-I *NA-207 Nawabshah-I == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-211
