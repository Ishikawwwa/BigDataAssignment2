Nizhnyaya Buzinovka () is a rural locality (a khutor) in Verkhnebuzinovskoye Rural Settlement, Kletsky District, Volgograd Oblast, Russia. The population was 65 as of 2010.Всероссийская перепись населения 2010 года.
