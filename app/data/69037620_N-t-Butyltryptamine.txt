N-t-Butyltryptamine (NTBT) is a tryptamine derivative which has serotonergic effects. It is described by Alexander Shulgin as producing "a light-headed intoxication that is a totally pleasant buzz, but nothing more profound than that" at a dosage range of 5 to 20 mg, along with the related sec-butyl isomer NSBT which is similar in effects but slightly less potent. ==See also== * alpha-Ethyltryptamine * 4-HO-DSBT * 4-HO-McPeT * 5-Chloro-αMT * MPMI ==References== Category:Tryptamines Category:Tert-butyl compounds Category:Secondary amines Category:Ethyleneamines
