NA-139 Pakpattan-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It mainly comprises Pakpattan Tehsil and includes a majority of the areas from the old NA-164 Pakpattan-I, while basically being an expanded version of the previous NA-165 Pakpattan-II. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-145 Pakpattan-I=== Election Member Party 2018 Ahmad Raza Maneka PML (N) == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002.
