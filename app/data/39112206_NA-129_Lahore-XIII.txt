NA-129 Lahore-XIII () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Farid Ahmad Paracha of Muttahida Majlis-e-Amal won by 30,326 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Mian Marghoob Ahmad of PML-N won by 72,227 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Mehar Ishtiaq Ahmad of PML-N won by 114,474 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections were held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-128 Lahore-XII *NA-130 Lahore-XIV == References == == External links == * Election result's official website NA-121
