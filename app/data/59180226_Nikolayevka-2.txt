Nikolayevka-2 () is a rural locality (a selo) in Nikolayevsky Selsoviet of Zeysky District, Amur Oblast, Russia. The population was 20 as of 2018. There are 7 streets. == Geography == Nikolayevka-2 is located 33 km southwest of Zeya (the district's administrative centre) by road.
