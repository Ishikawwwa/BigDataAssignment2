Newtowncunningham railway station served Newtowncunningham in County Donegal, Ireland. The station opened on 3 June 1883 when the Londonderry and Lough Swilly Railway built their line from Londonderry Graving Dock to Letterkenny (LLS). It closed for passengers on 3 June 1940.
