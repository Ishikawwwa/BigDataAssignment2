NA-210 Sanghar-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 October 2002. Liaqat Ali marri of PML-F won by 61,741 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 February 2008. Roshan din junejo of PML-F won by 71,394 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Roshan Din Junejo of PML-F won by 90,787 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-209 Sanghar-I *NA-211 Sanghar-III == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-236
