The N. J. Felix House is a historic home located in Asharoken, Suffolk County, New York. It was built about 1900 and is a -story, four-bay, shingled and clapboard residence with a steeply pitched hipped roof in an eclectic combination of the Queen Anne and Colonial Revival styles. It features two elongated decorative brick chimneys and gable dormers. It is a representative example of a large, upper-income single-family dwelling along Huntington's north shore. Also located on the property is a contributing privy.
