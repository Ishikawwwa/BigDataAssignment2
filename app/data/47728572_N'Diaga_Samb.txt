N'Diaga Samb (born August 6, 1966) is a SenegaleseSpelers Senegal: NDiaga Samb draughts player based in the Netherlands. He took first place at the African Championship in 2016,African Championship Bamako 2016 second place at the African Championship in 1992,Afrikaans Kampioenschap Abidjan 1992 2006,Afrikaans Kampioenschap Yaound 2006 2014,Championship of Africa Play Offs Niamey 2014 2018African Championship Thiès 2018 and finished third in 2003, 2009 and 2010. He won several international tournaments and was awarded the titles of International Master (MI) in 1992 and International Grandmaster (GMI)NDiaga Samb.
