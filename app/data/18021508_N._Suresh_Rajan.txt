Suresh Rajan (born 31 March 1963, Nagercoil) is an Indian politician from Tamil Nadu state. Mr.Suresh Rajan has a M.A. degree. He started his political career in the Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK) youth wing. He has been elected to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly for the years 1996–2001, 2006-2011 and from 2016.
