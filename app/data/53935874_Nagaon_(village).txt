Nagaon is a village in Barpeta district, situated in the north bank of the river Brahmaputra. ==Transport== The village is near Hajo Doulashal road (National Highway 427) and connected to nearby towns and cities like Barpeta, Hajo and Guwahati with regular buses and other modes of transportation. == Notable people == * Mahendra Mohan Choudhry, former Governor of Punjab and former Chief Minister of Assam ==See also== * Tukrapara * Tupamari ==References== Category:Villages in Barpeta district
