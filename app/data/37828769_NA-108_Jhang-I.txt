NA-108 Jhang-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It is based on the old NA-91 with the main difference being the transfer of the city of Ahmedpur Sial to the new NA-116. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-114 Jhang-I=== Election Member Party 2018 Sahabzada Muhammad Mehboob Sultan PTI ==Election 2002== General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Muhammad Mehboob Sultan of PML-Q won by 53,545 votes. ==Election 2008== General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Muhammad Mehboob Sultan of PML-Q won by 75,803 votes. ==Election 2013== General elections were held on 11 May 2013.
