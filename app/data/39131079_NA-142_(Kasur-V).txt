Constituency NA-142 (Kasur-V) () was a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It comprised mainly the city of Phool Nagar, which, according to the 2018 delimitations, has now been included in Constituency NA-140 (Kasur- IV). == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sardar Muhammad Asif Nakai of PML-Q won by 58,420 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Sardar Talib Hassan Nakai of PML-Q won by 47,192 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Rana Muhammad Hayat Khan of PML-N won by 65,758 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == References == == External links == * Election result's official website NA-142 Category:Abolished National Assembly Constituencies of Pakistan
