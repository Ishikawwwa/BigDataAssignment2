NGC 106 is a lenticular galaxy estimated to be about 270 million light-years away in the constellation of Pisces. It was discovered by Francis Leavenworth in 1886 and its apparent magnitude is 14.5. ==Notes== ==References== ==External links== * 0106 Category:Pisces (constellation) Category:Astronomical objects discovered in 1886 Category:Discoveries by Francis Leavenworth
