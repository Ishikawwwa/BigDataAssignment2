NA-143 Sahiwal-III () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Laghari of National Alliance won by 39,312 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Malik Noman Ahmed Langrial of PML-Q won by 39,864 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Chaudhry Muhammad Munir Azhar of PML-N won by 89,126 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == ==See also== *NA-142 Sahiwal-II *NA-144 Khanewal-I == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-163
