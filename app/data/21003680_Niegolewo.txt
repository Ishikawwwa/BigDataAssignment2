Niegolewo is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Opalenica, within Nowy Tomyśl County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately north of Opalenica, east of Nowy Tomyśl, and west of the regional capital Poznań. The village has a population of 344. ==References== Category:Villages in Nowy Tomyśl County
