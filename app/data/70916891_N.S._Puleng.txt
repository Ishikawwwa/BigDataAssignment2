N.S. Puleng (Samuel Puleng Nkomo) is a writer in the Northern Sotho language. He was born 10 January 1958 in South Africa, in the "Blacks only" township of Bengweni, which was next to Randfontein, which was a Whites-only town. His father was a missionary who had come from (then) Rhodesia as a Christian missionary. Because his father died early, he was raised by his mother, Sarah "Peggy" Priscilla Mmapoo, a nurse and midwife.p. 18. Phalane, Manthiba. "“Kgadi’a Bakone” NS Puleng." Almas Urdu Research Journal 8, no. 1 (2005): 12–19.
