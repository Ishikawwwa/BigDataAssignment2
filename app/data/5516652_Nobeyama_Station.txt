is a railway station on the Koumi Line in the village of Minamimaki in Nagano Prefecture, Japan, operated by the East Japan Railway Company (JR East). The station is the highest JR station in Japan. ==Lines== Nobeyama Station is served by the Koumi Line and is from the starting point of the line at Kobuchizawa Station. ==Station layout== The station consists of one side platform and one island platform, serving three tracks, connected by a level crossing. The station has a Midori no Madoguchi staffed ticket office. With the privatization of Japanese National Railways (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East.
