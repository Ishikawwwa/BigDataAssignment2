The Høyen Medal is one of several medals awarded by the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts. It is named after Niels Laurits Høyen, known as the first Danish art historian. The N. L. Høyen Medal is awarded to art historians and other theorists, arts administrators, educators and journalists. ==Recipients== ===2020s=== * 2020: Karsten Ifversen. ===2010s=== * 2019 Jan Haugaard *
