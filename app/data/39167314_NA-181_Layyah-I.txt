NA-181 Layyah-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sardar Bahadur Ahmad Khan of PML-Q won by 86,427 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Sardar Bahadur Ahmad Khan of PML-Q won by 58,797 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Sahibzada Faiz Ul Hassan of PML-N won by 119,403 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-180 Muzaffargarh-IV *NA-182 Layyah- II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-181
