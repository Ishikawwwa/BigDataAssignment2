NA-29 Peshawar-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. ==Area== During the delimitation of 2018, NA-28 (Peshawar-II) acquired most of its area from NA-4 (Peshawar-IV) with a very small part acquired from NA-1 (Peshawar-I).
