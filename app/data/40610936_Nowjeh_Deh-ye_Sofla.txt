Nowjeh Deh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Nowjeh Deh-ye Soflá; also known as Nowjeh Deh-ye Shojā‘ān, Nowjeh Deh Shoja’eyan, Nowjeh Deh-ye Pā’īn, Nowjeh Deh-ye Shojā‘ī, Nowjeh Deh-ye Shojā‘īān, Nowjeh Deh-ye Sojā’ī, and Nūjeh Deh-e Shojā‘īān) is a village in Misheh Pareh Rural District, in the Central District of Kaleybar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 146, in 43 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Kaleybar County
