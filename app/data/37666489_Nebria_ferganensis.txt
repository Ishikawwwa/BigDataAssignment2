Nebria ferganensis is a species of ground beetle in the Nebriinae subfamily that is endemic to Kyrgyzstan. ==Subspecies== The species bears 2 subspecies all of whom are endemic to Kyrgyzstan: *Nebria ferganensis ferganensis Shilenkov, 1982 *Nebria ferganensis urumbashi Kabak & Putchkov, 1996 ==References== ferganensis Category:Beetles described in 1982 Category:Beetles of Asia Category:Endemic fauna of Kyrgyzstan Category:Insects of Central Asia
