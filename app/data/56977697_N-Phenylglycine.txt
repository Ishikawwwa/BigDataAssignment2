N-Phenylglycine is an organic compound with the formula C6H5NHCH2CO2H. This white solid achieved fame as the industrial precursor to indigo dye. It is a non-proteinogenic alpha amino acid related to sarcosine, but with an N-phenyl group in place of N-methyl. ==Preparation== It is prepared by the Strecker reaction involving the reaction of formaldehyde, hydrogen cyanide, and aniline.
