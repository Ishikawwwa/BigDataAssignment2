National Highway 227F, commonly referred to as NH 227F is a national highway in Mehsi, India. It is a secondary route of National Highway 27. NH-227F runs in the state of Bihar in India. == Route == NH227F connects Mehsi (Madhuban) Phulwaria Ghat, Patahi and Bairgania in the state of Bihar. == Junctions == : Terminal near Mehsi. == See also == * List of National Highways in India * List of National Highways in India by state == References == == External links == * NH 227F on OpenStreetMap Category:National highways in India Category:National Highways in Bihar
