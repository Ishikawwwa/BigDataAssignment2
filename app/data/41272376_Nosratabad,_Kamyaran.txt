Nosratabad (, also Romanized as Noşratābād; also known as Tāzehābād-e Gerger) is a village in Amirabad Rural District, Muchesh District, Kamyaran County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 87, in 15 families.
