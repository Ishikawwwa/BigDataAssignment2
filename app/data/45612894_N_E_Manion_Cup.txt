The N E Manion Cup is an Australian Turf Club Group 3 Thoroughbred quality handicap horse race for three year olds and upwards over a distance of 2400 metres, held annually at Rosehill Racecourse in Sydney, Australia in March. Total prizemoney for the race is A$200,000. ==History== The race is named in honour of N. E.
