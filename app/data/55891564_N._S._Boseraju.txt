Nadimpalli. S. Boseraju is an Indian politician from Karnataka. He is currently serving as Cabinet Minister in Government of Karnataka. he was the member of the Karnataka Legislative Council. He was a two-time Member of Karnataka Legislative Assembly representing Manvi.He was born in a Telugu Kshatriya family at Mogallu Village , Palakoderu Mandal , Bhimavaram Town.
