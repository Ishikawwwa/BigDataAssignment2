Nicolas Galazzi (born 18 December 2000) is an Italian professional footballer who plays as a winger for club Brescia. ==Club career== Formed on Academia Pavese and Inter Milan, Galazzi was loaned to Serie D club Fanfulla in 2018, where he made his senior debut. He left Inter in 2019, and joined Piacenza. Piacenza loaned the player to Serie D club USD Vigor Carpaneto. In 2021, he signed for Venezia. He made his Serie A debut as a late substitute on 22 August 2021 against Napoli. On 28 August 2021, he was loaned to Triestina.
