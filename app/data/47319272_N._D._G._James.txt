Noel David Glaves James, known as "Jimmy," (1912-1993) was an English author and historian of forestry. His works included: * The Forester's Companion (1955) * A Pictorial Guide to Bicton Gardens (1970) * A History of English Forestry (1981) * A Forestry Centenary: the history of the Royal Forestry Society of England, Wales, and Northern Ireland (1982) * Plain Soldiering.
