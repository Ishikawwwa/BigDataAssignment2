N2-citryl-N6-acetyl-N6-hydroxylysine synthase (, N(alpha)-citryl-N(epsilon)-acetyl-N(epsilon)-hydroxylysine synthase, iucA (gene)) is an enzyme with systematic name citrate:N6-acetyl-N6-hydroxy-L- lysine ligase (ADP-forming). This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction : 2 ATP + citrate + N6-acetyl-N6-hydroxy-L-lysine + H2O \rightleftharpoons 2 ADP + 2 phosphate + N2-citryl-N6-acetyl-N6-hydroxy-L- lysine This enzyme requires Mg2+. == References == == External links == * Category:EC 6.3.2
