NA-170 Rahim Yar Khan-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Major(R) Syed Tanveer Hussain Syed of PPP won by 45,500 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Mian Abdul Sattar of PPP won by 58,572 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Sheikh Fayyaz Ud Din of PML-N won by 86,232 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-169 Rahim Yar Khan-I *NA-171 Rahim Yar Khan-III == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-193
