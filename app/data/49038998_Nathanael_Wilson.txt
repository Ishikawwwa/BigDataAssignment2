Nathanael Wilson (died 3 November 1695) was a 17th-century English Anglican priest in Ireland. Wilson was educated at Magdalen Hall, Oxford. He was Chaplain to James Butler, 1st Duke of Ormonde, Lord Lieutenant of Ireland before being appointed Dean of Raphoe in 1684.
