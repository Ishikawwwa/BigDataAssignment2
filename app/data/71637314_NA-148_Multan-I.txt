NA-148 Multan-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sikandar Bosan of PML-Q won by 47,368 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008.
