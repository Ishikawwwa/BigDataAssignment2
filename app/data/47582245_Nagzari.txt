Nagzari is a village in the Palghar district of Maharashtra, India. It is located in the Dahanu taluka. == Demographics == According to the 2011 census of India, Nagzari has 668 households. The effective literacy rate (i.e. the literacy rate of population excluding children aged 6 and below) is 43.3%.
