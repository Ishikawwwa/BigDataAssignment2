NA-142 Sahiwal-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Rana Tariq Javed (independent candidate) won by 42,885 votes. He then joined Pakistan Muslim League (Q) == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Ghulam Farid Kathia of PPP won by 38,926 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Chaudhary Muhammad Ashraf of PML-N won by 94,012 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == ==See also== *NA-141 Sahiwal-I *NA-143 Sahiwal-III == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-161
