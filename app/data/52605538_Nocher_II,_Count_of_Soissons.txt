Nocher II (died 1019), Count of Bar-sur-Aube, Count of Soissons. He was the son of Nocher I, Count of Bar-sur-Aube. Nocher's brother Beraud (d. 1052) was Bishop of Soissons. Nocher became Count of Soissons, jure uxoris, upon his marriage to Adelise, Countess of Soissons.
