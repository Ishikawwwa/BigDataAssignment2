Cristian Nahuel Maidana (born 7 April 1998) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Chacarita Juniors, on loan from Ferro Carril Oeste. ==Career== Maidana's senior career began with Ferro Carril Oeste, with the midfielder being moved into their senior squad during the 2018–19 season and initially being on the substitutes bench against Instituto and Temperley in February 2019. Interim manager Jorge Cordon selected Maidana for his senior bow on 3 March during a 2–0 victory at home to Mitre.
