Noir is the second studio album by the South Korean boy group B.A.P. It was released on November 7, 2016, under the label of TS Entertainment. "Skydive" was used as the lead single. ==Track listing== == Promotion == Promotions for the album started on November 11, 2016, at Mnet's M Countdown and continued onto KBS2's Music Bank, MBC's Show! Music Core, SBS's Inkigayo, SBS MTV's The Show, and MBC Music's Show Champion. ==Other news== Bang Yong-guk, the leader of B.A.P, took a hiatus from musical activities and did not participate in the promotions of this album due to health reasons. ==Charts== Chart (2016) Peak position Billboard World Albums 1 Gaon Album Chart 2 Oricon Albums Chart 39 == Awards and nominations == === Music program awards === Song Program Date "Skydive" The Show (SBS) November 22, 2016 == Release history == Region Date Format Label South Korea November 7, 2016 CD, digital download TS Entertainment Worldwide Digital download ==References== Category:2016 albums Category:B.A.P (South Korean band) albums Category:Kakao M albums Category:TS Entertainment albums
