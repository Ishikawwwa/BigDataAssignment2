N.L. Upadhyaya ( - 1989) was an India politician and trade unionist. In 1942 he founded the branch of the Communist Party of India in Mysore State. \- In 1964, he became a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India (Marxist). He was a member of the Karnataka State Secretariat of the party and vice president of the Coffee Board Labour Union.
