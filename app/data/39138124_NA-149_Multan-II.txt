NA-149 Multan-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Malik Liaqat Ali Dogar of PPP won by 31,085 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Javed Hashmi of PML-N won by 70,864 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Javed Hashmi of Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf won by 83,640 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == By-election 2014 == After Javed Hashmi's resignation from National Assembly, by election for this vacant seat were held on 16 October 2014. Javed Hashmi as an Independent candidate with support from PML-N lost to Amir Dogar, also an Independent candidate with Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf's backing. == Election 2018 == ==See also== *NA-148 Multan-I *NA-150 Multan-III == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-149
