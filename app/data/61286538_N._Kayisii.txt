N. Kayisii is an Indian politician elected to the Manipur Legislative Assembly from Tadubi in 2017 and 2022 Manipur Legislative Assembly election as a member of the National People's Party. He is the Manipur state unit president of the party since march 2021. He was Minister of Tribal and hill areas department and fisheries (2017-2020) in N.
