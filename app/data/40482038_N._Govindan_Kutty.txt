N. Govindan Kutty was a Malayalam film actor. He has appeared in around 100 films, acting mainly in villain roles. ==Background== Kutty was born at Fort Kochi in 1924, Ernakulam as the son of Sankara Narayanan and Nanukutty Amma. He was a natural actor and was prominent as a villain during the time when Prem Nazir and Jayan were heroes. He had written several professional dramas and screenplays for Malayalam films.
