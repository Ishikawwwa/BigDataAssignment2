NA-198 Ghotki-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Khalid Ahmed Khan Lund of PPP won by 68,296 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Mian Abdul Haq Alias Mian Mitho of PPP won by 59,022 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Ali Gohar Khan Mahar of PPP won by 86,579 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-197 Qambar Shahdadkot-II *NA-199 Ghotki-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-200
