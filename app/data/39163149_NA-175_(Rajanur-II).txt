Constituency NA-175 (Rajanpur-II) () was a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It comprised Rajanpur Tehsil and Rojhan Tehsil. After the 2018 delimitations, this constituency's areas were bifurcated between the new constituencies of NA-194 and NA-195. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sardar Nasrullah Khan Dreshak an Independent candidate won by 69,030 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Mir Dost Muhammad Mazari of PPP won by 78,427 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Dr. Hafeez Ur Rehman Drishak of PML-N won by 110,573 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-175 Category:Abolished National Assembly Constituencies of Pakistan
