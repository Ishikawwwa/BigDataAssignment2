Nikolay Yuryevich Boyarintsev (; born 16 April 1988) is a Russian former professional association football player. ==Club career== He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on 27 March 2008 in a game against FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk.
