NA-209 Sanghar-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Qazi Abdul Qudus Rajar of PML-F won by 61,671 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Ghulam Dastagir Rajar of PML-F won by 68,122 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Shazia Marri of PPP won and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-208 Nawabshah-II *NA-210 Sanghar-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-235
