Newton is a locality, farm and former feudal barony, in the parish of Forgan, south of Wormit in Fife, Scotland. ==History== Newton was held by the Lacelles family until the estate passed by the marriage of Marjorie Lascelles to Richard de Moravia in the 13th century.Fife Placenames - Newton The estate passed by marriage of the heiress Gyles Murray to Thomas Kinnaird, in the 15th century. ==References== Category:Baronies in the Baronage of Scotland
