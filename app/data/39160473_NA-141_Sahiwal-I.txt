NA-141 Sahiwal-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Chaudhry Noraiz Shakoor Khan of PPP won by 53,174 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Syed Imran Ahmed Shah of PML-N won by 59,373 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Syed Imran Ahmed Shah of PML-N won by 99,553 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-140 Pakpattan-II *NA-142 Sahiwal-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-160
