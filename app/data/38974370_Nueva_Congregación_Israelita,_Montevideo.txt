The Synagogue of the New Jewish Congregation (, often known by its acronym NCI) is a synagogue in Montevideo, Uruguay. ==Overview== The New Jewish Congregation was founded in 1936 mostly by German and Austrian Jews who fled Nazism. It established two synagogues, the big one on Wilson Ferreira Aldunate Street is Conservative, the smaller one was Orthodox.
