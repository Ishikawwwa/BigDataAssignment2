N. K. Sudhindra Rao is an Indian jurist known for his tenure as a judge on the Karnataka Lokayukta special court in Bangalore, which is tasked with dealing with political corruption. Rao has a reputation for upholding the values of the Lokayukta system, remaining unswayed by political pressure. ==Awards== Rao received the Namma Benagaluru Varshada Vyakthi award in 2011. ==References== Category:20th-century Indian judges Category:Living people Category:Ombudsmen in India Category:Year of birth missing (living people)
