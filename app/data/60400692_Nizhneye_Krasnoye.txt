Nizhneye Krasnoye () is a rural locality (a village) in Dobryansky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 15 as of 2010. There are 4 streets. == Geography == Nizhneye Krasnoye is located 70 km north of Dobryanka (the district's administrative centre) by road.
