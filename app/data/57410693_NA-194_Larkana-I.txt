NA-194 Larkana-I () is a newly created constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It mainly comprises the Ratodero Taluka and some areas of the Larkana Taluka, which includes a portion of the city of Larkana. It was created in the 2018 delimitation after the constituency overlapping between Qambar Shahdadkot District and Larkana District was ended. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-193 Shikarpur-II *NA-195 Larkana-II == References == Larkana
