Nostalgiya () is a Russian television channel, catering to nostalgia for the Soviet Union. Launched in 2004, the channel broadcasts to almost all the European portion of the former Soviet Union The logo of the channel is stylized with a combination of Cyrillic letters and that looks like the hammer and sickle (☭) turned in the opposite direction to the right.
