Nette is a river in Lower Saxony, Germany, a left tributary of the Innerste. The Nette rises in the Harz, in the municipality of Seesen. The Nette flows through Bockenem before reaching the Innerste in Holle, between Hildesheim and Salzgitter. ==See also== *List of rivers of Lower Saxony ==References== Category:Rivers of Lower Saxony Category:Rivers of Germany
