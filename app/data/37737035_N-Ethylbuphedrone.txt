N-Ethylbuphedrone (also known as NEB) is a stimulant of the cathinone class that has been sold as a designer drug. It is the β-ketone analogue of N,alpha- diethylphenylethylamine. ==Legal status== As of October 2015 NEB is a controlled substance in China.
