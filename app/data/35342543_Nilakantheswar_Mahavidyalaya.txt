Nilakantheswar Mahavidyalaya Degree Mahavidyalaya(NM) is in the South Balanda area of Talcher, Odisha, India. It was established in 1992. It was started as a tutorial college with the name of Nilakatheswar Vidyapeeth (+2) in 1987 to fulfill the dreams of higher education for the children of Mahanadi Coalfields Limited employees as well as local people.
