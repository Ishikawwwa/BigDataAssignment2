Nikolay Petrovich Osipov () (1751 in Saint Petersburg – in Saint Petersburg, Russian Empire) was a Russian writer, poet and translator. He is best known for his mock-heroic 1791 poem (; parts 5 and 6 were completed after his death by Aleksandr Kotelnitsky).
