Malik Allah Yar Khan of PML-Q won by 88,784 votes. === Election 2008 === Chaudhry Pervaiz Elahi succeeded in the election 2008 and became the member of National Assembly. === Election 2013 === General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Malik Atibar Khan of PML-N won by 85,244 votes and became the member of National Assembly. ===Election 2018=== General elections were held on 25 July 2018.
