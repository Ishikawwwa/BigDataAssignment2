NA-25 (Dera Ismail Khan-cum-Tank) () was a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. It was split into NA-37 (Tank) and NA-39 (Dera Ismail Khan-II) in 2018. The constituency was established as NA-25 (Dera Ismail Khan) in 1977 when Tank was part of Dera Ismail Khan District as one of its tehsils.
