NA-207 Nawabshah-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Dr. Azra Fazal Pechuho of PPP won by 75,237 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Dr. Azra Fazal Pechuho of PPP won by 108,404 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Dr. Azra Fazal Pechuho of PPP won by 113,199 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. ==See also== *NA-206 Naushahro Feroze-II *NA-208 Nawabshah-II == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-213
