NA-230 Karachi Malir-I () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-236 Karachi Malir-I=== Election Member Party 2018 Jam Abdul Karim Bijar PPPP == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Sher Muhammad Baloch of Pakistan Peoples Party won by 38,225 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Sher Muhammad Baloch of PPP won by 134,696 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013.
