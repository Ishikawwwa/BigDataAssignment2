Nikolaos "Nikos" Karabelas (; born 20 December 1984) is a Greek footballer who plays as a left back. ==Club career== Karabelas made his debut with his hometown's Paniliakos in the Beta Ethniki in 2004. Two years later he joined Superleague side Aris, and quickly established himself as a starter for the Yellows.
