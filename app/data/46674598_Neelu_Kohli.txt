Neelu Kohli (also Nilu Kohli ) is an Indian film and television actress. She has done many roles in many Indian television series, like Sangam, Naamkaran, Mere Angne Mein, Maddam Sir and Choti Sarrdaarni. She is also worked in Hindi films such as Housefull 2, Hindi Medium and Patiala House. ==Early life== She was born and brought up in Ranchi. Her mother was Meeta Duggal. She studied in Loreto Convent and Nirmala College. She was married to Harminder Singh Kohli who died in March 2023.
