N.Y. Hanumanthappa (born 25 September 1939) was a member of the 14th Lok Sabha of India. He represented the Chitradurga constituency of Karnataka and is a member of the Indian National Congress (INC) political party. Before his election to the Lok Sabha, Hanumanthappa served as chief justice of the Orissa High Court, having previously served on the High Courts of Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh.
