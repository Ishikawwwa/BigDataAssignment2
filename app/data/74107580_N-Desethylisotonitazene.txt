N-Desethylisotonitazene is a benzimidazole derivative with potent opioid effects which has been sold as a designer drug. It was first identified as an active metabolite of the related compound isotonitazene, but was unexpectedly found to be even more potent than the parent compound, and is among the most potent opioid agonists in this family, around 20x stronger than fentanyl. It has become an increasingly widespread drug of abuse in its own right, linked to numerous cases of drug overdose,and may be considered an analog of a schedule 1 drug in the USA.Malcolm NJ, Palkovic B, Sprague DJ, Calkins MM, Lanham JK, Halberstadt AL, Stucke AG, McCorvy JD.
