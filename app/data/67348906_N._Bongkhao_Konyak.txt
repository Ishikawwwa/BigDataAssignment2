N. Bongkhao Konyak is a politician from Nagaland. He was elected to the Nagaland Legislative Assembly in 2018 from Tobu Assembly constituency as candidate of Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party.N. Bongkhao Konyak (NDPP) : Constituency - TOBU He was appointed the Advisor for Department of Under Developed Areas (DUDA) in Fourth Neiphiu Rio ministry.
