Nathong Airport is a domestic airport that serves Xam Neua, the capital of Houaphan Province, Laos. ==Airlines and destinations== ==Accidents and incidents== * On October 19, 2000, Lao Aviation Flight 703 was crashed into the mountains near Nathong Airport, Xam Neua. 8 passengers died while 9 passengers and crews survived, but suffered injuries. ==References== Category:Airports in Laos
