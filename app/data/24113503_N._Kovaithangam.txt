N. Kovaithangam ( – 12 October 2022)Former MLA Coimbatore Thangam passed away due to ill health was an Indian politician. Born in India. He was elected to the Tamil Nadu legislative assembly as a Tamil Maanila Congress candidate from Valparai constituency in 2001, and 2006 elections. On 23 March he joined DMK in presence of party president M. K.
