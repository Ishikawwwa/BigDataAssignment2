N'Gangoro is a village in central Ivory Coast. It is in the sub-prefecture of Tiébissou, Tiébissou Department, Bélier Region, Lacs District. Until 2002, N'Gangoro was in the commune of N'Gangoro-Attouto. In March 2012, N'Gangoro- Attouto became one of 1126 communes nationwide that were abolished."Le gouvernement ivoirien supprime 1126 communes, et maintient 197 pour renforcer sa politique de décentralisation en cours", news.abidjan.net, 7 March 2012. ==Notes== Category:Populated places in Lacs District Category:Populated places in Bélier
