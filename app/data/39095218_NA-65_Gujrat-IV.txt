NA-65 (Gujrat-IV) () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-69 Gujrat-II=== Election Member Party 2018 Chaudhry Pervaiz Elahi PML (Q) By-election 2018 Moonis Elahi PML (Q) == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Chaudhry Shujaat Hussain of PML-Q won by 66,809 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Chaudhry Ahmed Mukhtar of PPP won by 79,735 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Chaudhary Pervez Elahi of PML-Q won by 78,171 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections were held on 25 July 2018.
