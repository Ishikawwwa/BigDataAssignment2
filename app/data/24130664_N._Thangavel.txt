N. Thangavel (died 8 July 1997) was an Indian politician and Member of the Legislative Assembly of Tamil Nadu. He was elected to the Tamil Nadu legislative assembly as a Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam candidate from Coonoor constituency in the 1989, and 1996 elections.
