N. Ch. Joseph was the President of the Protestant Andhra Evangelical Lutheran Church Society. Although Joseph's period was short-lived, he undertook the mantle of the President to instill confidence in the ecclesiastical community. N. Ch. Joseph belonged to the senior clergy of the Andhra Evangelical Lutheran Church Society.
