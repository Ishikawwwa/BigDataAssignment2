Nasir Colony () is a neighbourhood in the Korangi District in eastern Karachi, Pakistan. It was previously part of Korangi Town,Korangi Town - Government of Karachi which was an administrative unit that was disbanded in 2011. There are several ethnic groups in Nasir Colony including Muhajirs, Punjabis, Sindhis, Kashmiris, Seraikis, Pakhtuns, Balochis, Memons, Christians, Bohras and Ismailis. Nasir Colony has two large mosques, one church, and one football pitch.
