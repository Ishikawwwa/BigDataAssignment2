The College of Engineering and Physical-Mathematic Sciences of the National Polytechnic Institute is a highly recognized institute of higher education in Mexico. The College was proposed in 1932 by Secretary of Public Education Narciso Bassols and was founded by integrating the already existent School of Mechanical and Electrical Sciences that dates back to the 19th century.
