Nguyễn Đỗ Cung (1912 - 22 September 1977) was a Vietnamese artist. He was a student of EBAI in Hanoi. In 1946, he was one of the first, with Tô Ngọc Vân and Nguyễn Thị Kim to make portraits of Ho Chi Minh.Vietnam Fine Arts Museum - Bảo tàng mỹ thuật Việt Nam - 2000 "Nguyễn Đỗ Cung was a painter whose heart was filled with hidden sadness and qualms before the Revolution.
