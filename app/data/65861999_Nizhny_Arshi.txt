Nizhny Arshi (; Dargwa: УбяхӀ ГӀярши) is a rural locality (a selo) in Arshimakhinsky Selsoviet, Levashinsky District, Republic of Dagestan, Russia. The population was 548 as of 2010. There are 8 streets.Село Нижний Арши на карте == Geography == Nizhny Arshi is located 26 km southwest of Levashi (the district's administrative centre) by road.
