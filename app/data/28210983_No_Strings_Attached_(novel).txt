No Strings Attached is the 170th volume in the Nancy Drew Mystery Stories series.No Strings Attached at WorldCat ==Plot summary== The sleuthing friends travel to Paris (where several previous cases have also developed). George has organized some tennis matches there, and Nancy and Bess tag along in hopes of enjoying the sightseeing. The rooming house where they stay is owned by Mimi Louseau, a 37-year-old puppeteer and museum proprietor.
