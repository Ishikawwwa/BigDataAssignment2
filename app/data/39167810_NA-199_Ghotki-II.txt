NA-199 Ghotki-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002.Ali Mohammad Mahar of PPP won by 77,950 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Ali Mohammad Mahar of PPP won by 74,714 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Ali Mohammad Mahar of PPP won by 124,472 votes and became the member of National Assembly. == Election 2018 == General elections are scheduled to be held on 25 July 2018. == By-election 2019 == The seat had fallen vacant following the death of former federal minister and father of Ahmad Ali, Ali Mohammad Khan Mahar ==See also== *NA-198 Ghotki-I *NA-200 Sukkur-I == References == == External links == *Election result's official website NA-201
