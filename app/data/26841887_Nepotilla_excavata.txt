Nepotilla excavata is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Raphitomidae. ==Description== The length of the shell varies between 1.5 mm and 2 mm. ==Distribution== This marine species is endemic to Australia and occurs off Tasmania, New South Wales, Victoria and South Australia. ==References== * Gatliff, J.H. 1906.
