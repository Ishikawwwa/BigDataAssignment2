Nothweiler is a municipality in Südwestpfalz district, in Rhineland- Palatinate, western Germany. The mayor is Nicole Grüny. As of December 2020 the population was 132. It is situated 268 metres above sea level and has an area of 3.68 km2 (1.42 sq mi). ==References== Category:Municipalities in Rhineland-Palatinate Category:Palatinate Forest Category:South Palatinate Category:Südwestpfalz
