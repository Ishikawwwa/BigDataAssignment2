N-Cyclohexyl-2-pyrrolidone or CHPChem Blink is a yellow to colorless liquid. It has a low vapor pressure, and is nearly odorless.BASF the chemical company It has a low solubility in water, but is soluble in a variety of organic solvents. CHP is used in the electronics industry as a photoresist stripper (usually in combination with other solvents like N-methyl-2-pyrrolidone), and as a chemical polisher of copper in circuit board fabrication.
