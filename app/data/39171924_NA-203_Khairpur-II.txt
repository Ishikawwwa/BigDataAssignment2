NA-203 Khairpur-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct 2002. Pir Sadaruddin Shah of PML-F won by 63,520 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Pir Sadaruddin Shah of PML-F won by 97,347 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Pir Sadaruddin Shah of PML-F won by 86,982 votes and became the member of National Assembly.
