N'écoutez pas, mesdames ! (Don't Listen, Ladies!) is a three-act comedy by Sacha Guitry, premiered at the Théâtre de la Madeleine on 23 May 1942. == Original cast == * Sacha Guitry : Daniel Bacheley * Hélène Perdrière : Madeleine Bacheley * Jeanne Fusier-Gir : Julie Bille-en-bois * Léon Walther : le baron de Charançay ==Revivals== The play was given as Don't Listen, Ladies at the St James's Theatre, London, in September 1948 with a cast including Constance Cummings, Denholm Elliott and Betty Marsden,"Don't Listen, Ladies", The Stage, 19 August 1948, p. 4 and at the Booth Theatre, Broadway, in December 1948 with a cast headed by Jack Buchanan."Don't Listen, Ladies" , Internet Broadway Database.
