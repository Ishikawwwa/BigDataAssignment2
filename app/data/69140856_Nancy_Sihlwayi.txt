Nomadewuka Nancy Sihlwayi is a South African politician, who was elected to the National Assembly of South Africa in the 2019 general elections as a member of the African National Congress. She had previously served as the Member of the Executive Council (MEC) responsible for the Department of Social Development in the Eastern Cape provincial government. Sihlwayi currently serves as a member of the Portfolio Committee on Human Settlements.
