Nakamura also has relatives who are kabuki actors. *Great-grandfather: Nakamura Kanemon II. *Grandfather: Nakamura Kanemon III. *Father: Nakamura Umenosuke IV. His trade name is and debuted in 1965. Nakamura learned about kabuki and modern theater at the Zenshinza troupe. His initial name was . Nakamura took on the name of his great- grandfather Baijaku in 1980 during the 50th Anniversary Performance of Zenshinza and became "Baijaku II". At the final performance of Otose in 2007, Nakamura announced he was leaving Zenshinza and turning freelance.
