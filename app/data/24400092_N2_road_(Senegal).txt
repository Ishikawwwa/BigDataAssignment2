The N2 road is one of the national roads of Senegal. It connects the west and the east of the country in a large arc following the northern frontier from the outskirts of the capital Dakar via Thiès, Louga, Saint-Louis, Richard Toll, Thilogne, Ouro Sogui, Kidira and Nayé on the border with Mali. ==See also== * N1 road * N3 road * N4 road * N5 road * N6 road * N7 road * Transport in Senegal Category:Road transport in Senegal
