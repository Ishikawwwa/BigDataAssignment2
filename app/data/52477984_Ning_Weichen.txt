Ning Weichen (; born 18 March 1996) is a Chinese footballer who currently plays for China League Two club Zhuhai Qin'ao. ==Club career== Ning Weichen moved to Portugal in 2014 and received training with Mafra and Loures youth academy. He made his senior debut on for Loures in the last of match of the season against Atlético da Malveira on 15 May 2016. Ning transferred to Cova da Piedade, which newly earned promotion to LigaPro, on 6 August 2016. On 27 November 2016, he made his debut for the club in a 2–1 home defeat against Famalicão, coming on as a substitute for Danielson in the 80th minute. Ning was loaned to Campeonato de Portugal side Tourizense in February 2017. On 14 July 2017, Ning transferred to Chinese Super League club Beijing Sinobo Guoan.
