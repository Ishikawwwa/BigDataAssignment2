Novokonstantinovka () is a rural locality (a selo) in Mirnovsky Selsoviet, Blagovarsky District, Bashkortostan, Russia. The population was 176 as of 2010. There are 2 streets. == Geography == Novokonstantinovka is located 25 km northwest of Yazykovo (the district's administrative centre) by road.
