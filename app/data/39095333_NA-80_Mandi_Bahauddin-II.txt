NA-80 Mandi Bahauddin-II () is a constituency for the National Assembly of Pakistan. ==Members of Parliament== ===2018-2022: NA-86 Mandi Bahauddin-II=== Election Member Party 2018 Nasir Iqbal Bosal PML (N) == Election 2002 == General elections were held on 10 Oct, 2002. Major(R) Zulfiqar Ali Gondal of PPP won by securing 71,424 votes. == Election 2008 == General elections were held on 18 Feb 2008. Nazar Muhammad Gondal of PPP won by 74,163 votes. == Election 2013 == General elections were held on 11 May 2013. Nasir Iqbal Bosal from Pakistan Muslim League (N) won by scoring 1,33,128 votes. Nazar Muhammad Gondal of Pakistan Peoples Party was runner-up who secured 43,574 votes. == Election 2018 == General elections were held on 25 July 2018.
