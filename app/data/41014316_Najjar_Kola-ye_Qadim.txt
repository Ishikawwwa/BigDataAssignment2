Najjar Kola-ye Qadim (, also Romanized as Najjār Kolā-ye Qadīm; also known as Najjār Kolā) is a village in Talarpey Rural District, in the Central District of Simorgh County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 697, in 196 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Simorgh County
