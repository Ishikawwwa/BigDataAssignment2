Netzahualcóyotl de la Vega García (18 January 1931 – 6 September 2004) was a Mexican trade union leader and politician affiliated with the Institutional Revolutionary Party. He served as Senator of the LIV, LV and LVIII Legislatures of the Mexican Congress representing Guerrero and as Deputy of the LII and LVI Legislatures.
