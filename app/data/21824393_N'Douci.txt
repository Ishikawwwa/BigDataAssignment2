N'Douci (also spelled Ndouci) is a town in southern Ivory Coast. It is a sub- prefecture of Tiassalé Department in Agnéby-Tiassa Region, Lagunes District. N'Douci was a commune until March 2012, when it became one of 1126 communes nationwide that were abolished."Le gouvernement ivoirien supprime 1126 communes, et maintient 197 pour renforcer sa politique de décentralisation en cours", news.abidjan.net, 7 March 2012.
