Edward "Ned" Gordon (born June 13, 1948) is an American politician from the state of New Hampshire. A Republican, Gordon has represented the 9th Grafton district in the New Hampshire House of Representatives from 2018 until 2022. He previously served one term in the State House and four in the New Hampshire Senate. He served as a Circuit Court Judge in the New Hampshire Court System from 2005 to 2018. He has served as Town Moderator of Bristol, New Hampshire since 1994. He has a BA and JD from the University of New Hampshire, and a MBA from Boston College.
