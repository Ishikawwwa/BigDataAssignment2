Nicolaes van Helt Stockade (1614–1669), was a Dutch Golden Age painter. ==Biography== thumb|left|300px|Mourning Phaëton, painted in 1650-60. According to Houbraken, Joost van den Vondel made a poem about him. Nikolaas de Helt Stokade in De groote schouburgh der Nederlantsche konstschilders en schilderessen (1718) by Arnold Houbraken, courtesy of the Digital library for Dutch literature Stockade served as a court painter to King Louis XIII between 1637 and 1645.
